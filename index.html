Alluri Seetharama Raju

Biography

Alluri Seetharama Raju was a revolutionary freedom fighter who played a crucial role during the Indian independence movement. Born on July 4, 1897, in Pandrangi, Andhra Pradesh, he became a symbol of resistance against British rule.

Known for his fearlessness and dedication to the cause of freedom, Alluri Seetharama Raju led the Rampa Rebellion against the British authorities. His charismatic leadership and guerrilla warfare tactics made him a hero among the people.

Despite facing many challenges, Alluri Seetharama Raju continued to fight for the rights of the oppressed. He became a legend in the history of Indian independence, inspiring generations to come.

Legacy

Alluri Seetharama Raju's legacy lives on, and he is remembered as a valiant freedom fighter who sacrificed his life for the cause of a free and independent India.
